# Privacy Policy — Flux Frog

**Last updated:** April 23, 2026
**Developer:** Reis Sa
**Contact:** reissal3000@gmail.com

Flux Frog is a free Android puzzle game in which you rotate pipes to guide water to the frogs. We value your privacy and collect as little data as possible. This policy explains what information is processed, how, and why.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Data stored locally on your device (never sent to any server)

- Current level number and progress
- Sound and music preferences (muted/unmuted)

This data is saved exclusively in your device's internal storage so the app can remember your progress and settings between sessions. It never leaves your device, is never sent to us or any third party, and is automatically deleted if you uninstall the app.

### Data collected automatically by Google AdMob (advertising)

We use Google AdMob to display banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded ads. AdMob may collect the following information:

- Advertising identifier (Advertising ID)
- Device information (model, manufacturer, Android version, language)
- App usage data (ad interactions, view duration)
- Approximate location (derived from IP address)
- Other technical data necessary to serve and measure ads

This data is used by Google to personalize ads, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud.

You can opt out of personalized ads in your Android settings (**Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization**) or reset your Advertising ID at any time.

**We do not collect or have access to:**

- Name, email address, phone number, or any directly identifiable personal data
- Photos, contacts, precise location, microphone, camera, or other device sensors
- Browsing history outside the app

## 3. Your Rights (LGPD — Brazil's General Data Protection Law)

Under the LGPD, you have the right to:

- Confirm the existence of data processing
- Access your data (though we collect very little)
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request anonymization, blocking, or deletion
- Withdraw consent (where applicable)
- File a complaint with the ANPD (Autoridade Nacional de Proteção de Dados)

Since we collect almost nothing directly identifiable, most of these rights apply to Google AdMob. To exercise rights related to AdMob data, visit your Google account settings or contact Google support.

For any questions or requests directed at us, email: **reissal3000@gmail.com**
